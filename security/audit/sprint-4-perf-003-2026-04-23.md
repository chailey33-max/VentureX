## Changes Implemented

3. Added responsive/perf-profiled visual fallbacks:
   - glassmorphism layers become opaque/translucent non-blur surfaces on lite profiles
   - modal overlays and card chrome switch to simpler paint paths for low-power scenarios
4. Preserved desktop visual quality:
   - high-fidelity profile retains original blur, texture, and pulse treatments
   - no business logic or entitlement/auth behavior changed
